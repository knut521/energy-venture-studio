## 1. Customer Segments

- **Early Adopters**: Environmentally conscious homeowners aged 35-55, living in urban and suburban areas, with a medium to high income, and a strong interest in sustainable living.
- **Mass Market**: Homeowners of all ages, living in both urban and rural areas, with a medium income, and a moderate interest in energy efficiency.
- **Market Size**: Estimated 10 million homeowners in the European Union, with a growth rate of 5% per annum.
- **Key Characteristics**: Homeowners who value convenience, ease of use, and cost savings, and are willing to invest in energy-efficient solutions.

## 2. Problem

- **Top Problem 1**: High energy bills, resulting from inefficient energy usage and lack of control over energy consumption.
- **Top Problem 2**: Limited access to energy-efficient solutions, leading to a lack of choice and high costs.
- **Top Problem 3**: Complexity in understanding and implementing energy-efficient solutions, making it difficult for homeowners to make informed decisions.
- **Existing Alternatives**: Current energy-efficient solutions, such as smart thermostats and energy monitors, are often expensive, complex to install, and provide limited functionality.

## 3. Unique Value Proposition

- **Primary UVP**: Our energy-efficient solution provides a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for homeowners to monitor, control, and optimize their energy consumption, resulting in significant cost savings and a reduced carbon footprint.
- **Differentiation**: Our solution is unique in its ability to integrate with various energy sources, providing a seamless and efficient energy management experience.
- **Proof Points**: Our solution has been tested and proven to reduce energy consumption by up to 30% and save homeowners an average of €500 per year.

## 4. Solution

- **Key Feature 1**: Energy monitoring and control system that provides real-time data on energy consumption and allows homeowners to set targets and receive alerts.
- **Key Feature 2**: Integration with various energy sources, including solar panels, wind turbines, and energy storage systems.
- **Key Feature 3**: Automated energy-saving recommendations based on weather forecasts, occupancy patterns, and energy usage history.
- **Minimum Viable Product**: A smart thermostat that can be controlled remotely and provides energy usage data.
