## Customer Journey Maps for Home Energy Apps

### Stage 1: Awareness & Consideration (Pre-Purchase)

* Customer Goal: Understand energy management requirements before purchasing a home energy app
* Touchpoints: Manufacturer websites, social media, online forums, word-of-mouth recommendations, energy provider recommendations
* Emotions: Curiosity, frustration, hope, skepticism, overwhelm
* Behaviors: Researching multiple energy apps, comparing features and prices, reading user reviews and ratings

### Stage 2: Acquisition & Setup (0-3 months)

* Customer Goal: Acquire and set up a home energy app
* Touchpoints: Online purchase process, order tracking, installation instructions, customer support
* Emotions: Excitement, anxiety, relief, satisfaction, frustration
* Behaviors: Testing the app, monitoring energy usage, adjusting settings, seeking support

### Stage 3: Ongoing Use (3+ months)

* Customer Goal: Continuously monitor and optimize energy usage
* Touchpoints: Regular app updates, new feature releases, energy efficiency tips, customer support
* Emotions: Engagement, motivation, satisfaction, frustration, overwhelm
* Behaviors: Regularly reviewing energy usage, adjusting settings, seeking support, exploring new features
