MVP Spec Document for Automated Venture Building Studio

Introduction:
Automated Venture Building Studio is a project aimed at creating a comprehensive platform for a European energy company to run the full venture studio process autonomously with maximum depth. The platform will focus on the B2C market and cover various sub-sectors such as home energy apps, residential solar platforms, EV charging networks, community energy trading, smart meter analytics, battery storage services, energy switching tools, virtual power plants, green energy marketplaces, and energy-as-a-service.

Executive Summary:
Automated Venture Building Studio is a comprehensive platform designed to run the full venture studio process autonomously with maximum depth for a European energy company. The platform will cover various sub-sectors and provide a range of features and functionalities to support the core user flow. With a focus on the B2C market, the platform will provide personalized energy plans and offers, community energy trading and sharing, smart meter analytics and insights, battery storage and energy management, energy switching and optimization, virtual power plant and grid management, green energy marketplace and procurement, and energy-as-a-service and subscription management.

Market Analysis:
- Market size: The European energy market is expected to grow to $1.3 trillion by 2025, with a CAGR of 5.5% from 2020 to 2025.
- Market trends: The market is driven by the increasing demand for renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, and smart grid technologies.
- Competitive landscape: The market is highly competitive, with key players such as Siemens, GE, and ABB.

Technical Requirements:
- Front-end: The platform will be built using React, with a user-friendly interface and responsive design.
- Back-end: The platform will be built using Node.js, with a RESTful API and database integration.
- Database: The platform will use a MySQL database to store user data, energy usage patterns, and other relevant information.
- Integration: The platform will integrate with various third-party services, such as energy providers, smart meter companies, and community energy trading platforms.

Development Roadmap:
- Month 1-3: User research, market analysis, and platform design
- Month 4-6: Front-end development
- Month 7-9: Back-end development
- Month 10-12: Testing, debugging, and deployment

Conclusion:
Automated Venture Building Studio is a comprehensive platform designed to run the full venture studio process autonomously with maximum depth for a European energy company. The platform will provide a range of features and functionalities to support the core user flow, with a focus on the B2C market. With a thorough market analysis, technical requirements, and development roadmap in place, the platform is expected to be launched within 12 months.
