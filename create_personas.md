## Daily Routine

Persona 1: Energy-Conscious Homeowner

* Wakes up at 7:00 AM to start the day
* Checks the home energy app to see how much energy has been used overnight
* Makes a cup of coffee and starts getting ready for work
* Leaves for work at 8:00 AM

Persona 2: EV Owner

* Wakes up at 6:30 AM to start the day
* Checks the home energy app to see how much energy has been used overnight
* Charges the electric vehicle and starts getting ready for work
* Leaves for work at 7:30 AM

Persona 3: Energy Entrepreneur

* Wakes up at 5:30 AM to start the day
* Checks the home energy app to see how much energy has been used overnight
* Reviews the company's energy usage and makes adjustments as needed
* Leaves for work at 6:30 AM

## Frustrations

Persona 1: Energy-Conscious Homeowner

* Frustrated with high energy bills

* Frustrated with complex installation processes

Persona 2: EV Owner

* Frustrated with faulty charging stations
* Frustrated with app fragmentation
* Frustrated with payment complexity

Persona 3: Energy Entrepreneur

* Frustrated with high energy costs
* Frustrated with lack of energy efficiency
* Frustrated with complex installation processes

## Trigger Moments

* Learning about new energy-efficient technologies

Persona 2: EV Owner

* Running out of charge
* Experiencing long wait times at charging stations

Persona 3: Energy Entrepreneur

* Seeing high energy bills
