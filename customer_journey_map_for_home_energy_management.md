## Customer Journey Map for Home Energy Management

## Executive Summary
This customer journey map provides a detailed visualization of the customer experience for home energy management applications. Based on extensive research of major players like Sense Energy Monitor, Emporia Energy, Zerofy, Homey, and Franklin Whole Home Battery systems, this map identifies key stages, touchpoints, emotions, and behaviors throughout the customer lifecycle.

## Customer Persona: Energy-Conscious Homeowner
- **Demographics**: Homeowner aged 35-55, tech-savvy, environmentally conscious
- **Goals**: Reduce energy bills, lower carbon footprint, gain energy insights
- **Pain Points**: High electricity costs, lack of visibility into energy usage, complex installation processes
- **Motivations**: Financial savings, environmental responsibility, home automation

## Customer Journey Stages
### Stage 1: Awareness (0-2 weeks)
**Customer Goal**: Discover solutions for energy monitoring and cost reduction

**Touchpoints**:
1. Google search for 'energy saving home apps'
2. Social media ads from energy monitoring companies
3. Word-of-mouth recommendations from friends/family
4. Energy provider recommendations
5. Home improvement store displays
6. Smart home technology blogs and reviews
7. YouTube tutorials on energy monitoring
8. Reddit communities (r/homeautomation, r/energy)
9. App store search results
10. Energy efficiency articles in mainstream media

**Emotions**:
- Curiosity about potential savings
- Frustration with high energy bills
- Hope for finding a solution
- Skepticism about claims
- Overwhelm from too many options

**Behaviors**:
- Researching multiple energy apps
- Comparing features and prices
- Reading user reviews and ratings
- Watching demo videos
- Asking questions in online forums
- Bookmarking promising solutions

### Stage 2: Consideration (1-3 weeks)
**Customer Goal**: Evaluate specific home energy apps and make selection decision

**Touchpoints**:
1. App feature comparison websites
2. Detailed product specifications
3. Customer support inquiries
4. Free trial sign-ups
5. Demo videos and webinars
6. Installation requirement documentation
7. Compatibility checking with existing smart home devices
8. Cost-benefit analysis calculators
9. Warranty and return policy review
10. Professional installer consultations

**Emotions**:
- Analysis paralysis from too many choices
- Concern about installation complexity
- Anxiety about making wrong choice
- Excitement about potential benefits
- Impatience to start saving money

**Behaviors**:
- Creating shortlists of top contenders
- Contacting customer support with questions
- Testing free versions when available
- Measuring electrical panel space
- Researching installation requirements
- Calculating ROI projections
- Reading detailed user manuals

### Stage 3: Purchase & Installation (1-4 days)
**Customer Goal**: Acquire and set up the energy monitoring system

**Touchpoints**:
1. Online purchase process
2. Order confirmation and shipping updates
3. Installation and setup instructions
4. Customer support for installation issues
5. Integration with existing smart home devices
6. Energy usage tracking and insights
7. Automated energy-saving recommendations
8. In-app notifications and updates
9. Customer support for ongoing issues
10. Regular software updates and feature releases

**Emotions**:
- Relief from high energy bills
- Satisfaction with energy savings
- Confidence in energy management
- Excitement about new features and updates
- Frustration with installation issues

**Behaviors**:
- Monitoring energy usage and savings
- Adjusting energy consumption habits
- Using automated energy-saving recommendations
- Integrating with other smart home devices
- Providing feedback and reviews

### Stage 4: Ongoing Use (1-6 months)
**Customer Goal**: Continuously monitor and improve energy management

**Touchpoints**:
1. Regular energy usage tracking
2. Automated energy-saving recommendations
3. In-app notifications and updates
4. Customer support for ongoing issues
5. Integration with other smart home devices
6. Energy efficiency articles and blog posts
7. Social media communities and forums
8. Energy-related podcasts and videos
9. Energy-saving challenges and contests
10. Customer support for ongoing issues

**Emotions**:
- Satisfaction with energy savings
- Confidence in energy management
- Excitement about new features and updates
- Frustration with installation issues
- Relief from high energy bills

**Behaviors**:
- Monitoring energy usage and savings
- Adjusting energy consumption habits
- Using automated energy-saving recommendations
- Integrating with other smart home devices
- Providing feedback and reviews
