Research on home energy apps has shown that the market is growing rapidly, with a projected size of $10.38 billion by 2030. The demand for energy usage analytics apps is increasing, with a CAGR of 16.4% from 2023 to 2030. The market is driven by technological advancements in communication protocols and device connectivity, as well as the prevalence of Wi-Fi and low-power wireless technologies. Software platforms that enable users to monitor and control their home energy systems through mobile apps or centralized dashboards are becoming increasingly popular. The adoption of smart energy apps and household energy practices is also on the rise, with a study showing that Norwegian households are adopting and using these apps to explore their potential.
