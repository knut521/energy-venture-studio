## Customer Pain Points

1. **Installation Complexity**: Difficult setup processes for whole-home monitors
2. **Data Accuracy**: Inconsistent or inaccurate energy measurements
3. **App Fragmentation**: Multiple apps needed for different energy systems
4. **Cost Barriers**: High upfront costs for comprehensive monitoring
5. **Technical Support**: Limited customer support resources
6. **Integration Challenges**: Difficulty connecting with existing smart home systems
7. **Data Overload**: Too much information without actionable insights

## Market Gaps and Opportunities

1. **Simplified Installation**: Plug-and-play solutions with guided setup
2. **Unified Platforms**: Single app for all energy management needs
3. **Actionable Insights**: Clear recommendations for energy savings
4. **Affordable Options**: Lower-cost solutions for mass market adoption
