### Pain Point Heatmap

**High Intensity Pain Points**:
- Comparison complexity (overwhelming options)
- Switching delays (beyond 5-day target)
- Hidden fees and charges
- Information accuracy issues

**Medium Intensity Pain Points**:
- Customer service gaps
- Greenwashing concerns
- Digital accessibility
- Price change uncertainty

**Low Intensity Pain Points**:
- Documentation overload
- Payment method limitations
- Mobile app functionality
- Multi-fuel coordination

## Performance Metrics Dashboard

**Switching Metrics**:
- Completion Rate: 85% within 5 days
- Satisfaction Score: 80% positive experience
- Compensation Rate: <5% requiring claims
- Retention Rate: 70% after first contract
- Advocacy Score: 40% recommendation rate

**Customer Experience Metrics**:
- Time to Decision: Average 2.5 weeks
- Comparison Complexity: Reduction target 50%
- Communication Quality: 90% satisfaction
- Digital Accessibility: 95% success rate
- Error Rate: <2% billing discrepancies

## Regulatory Compliance Framework

**Ofgem Requirements**:
- Faster Switching Programme standards
- £40 compensation for eligible delays
- 14-day cooling-off period
- Clear price change communications
- Accurate billing processes
- Transparent tariff information

**Customer Rights**:
- Right to switch without exit fees (under certain conditions)
- Right to accurate meter readings
- Right to timely refunds
- Right to clear contract terms
- Right to complaint resolution
