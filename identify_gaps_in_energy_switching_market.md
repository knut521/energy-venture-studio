## Introduction
The energy switching market is a growing industry, with various players offering a range of solutions. However, despite the growth, there are still gaps in the market that need to be addressed.

## Customer Pain Points
1. High energy bills and the desire to reduce energy consumption
2. Lack of awareness about energy-efficient practices and technologies
3. Difficulty in finding and installing energy-efficient products and solutions

## Gaps in the Market
1. Limited availability of energy switching tools for residential use
2. Limited standardization of energy switching tools
3. Limited investment in energy switching technology

## Conclusion
In conclusion, the energy switching market has several gaps that need to be addressed. These gaps include the limited availability of energy switching tools for residential use, limited standardization of energy switching tools, and limited investment in energy switching technology. Addressing these gaps can provide opportunities for growth and innovation in the market.
