## Customer Journey Stages for European Energy Company
The customer journey for the European energy company can be broken down into four stages:
1. Awareness: The customer becomes aware of the product or service through various marketing channels such as social media, online advertising, and word-of-mouth.
2. Consideration: The customer considers purchasing the product or service by evaluating its features, benefits, and pricing.
3. Purchase: The customer makes a purchase and becomes a customer of the European energy company.
4. Post-Purchase: The customer receives support and maintenance after purchasing, including customer service, technical support, and energy management services.

These stages were identified using tools like Miro to create a visual representation of the journey. The output is a list of stages in the customer journey, which is saved in a file named 'customer_journey_stages.md'.
