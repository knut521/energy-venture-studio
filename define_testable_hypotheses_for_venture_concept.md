Define Testable Hypotheses for Venture Concept
============================================
### Introduction
Based on the research findings from the automated venture building studio for a European energy company (primarily B2C), this document defines testable hypotheses for the venture concept.

### Hypotheses
1. **Hypothesis 1**: We believe that implementing a home energy app will increase customer engagement by 20% within the next 6 months.
2. **Hypothesis 2**: We believe that investing in residential solar platforms will reduce carbon emissions by 15% within the next year.
3. **Hypothesis 3**: We believe that developing an EV charging network will increase the adoption of electric vehicles by 30% within the next 2 years.

### Expected Outcomes
* Increased customer engagement through home energy apps
* Reduced carbon emissions through residential solar platforms
* Increased adoption of electric vehicles through EV charging networks

### Assumptions
* Customers will adopt and use home energy apps
* Residential solar platforms will be cost-effective and efficient
* EV charging networks will be convenient and accessible
