## User Personas for Home Energy Apps

### Persona 1: Energy-Conscious Homeowner

* Age: 35-55
* Income: $50,000 - $100,000
* Education: College-educated
* Occupation: White-collar professional
* Values: Environmental sustainability, energy efficiency, cost savings
* Goals: Reduce energy consumption, lower utility bills, increase home value
* Pain Points: High energy bills, lack of visibility into energy usage, complex installation processes

### Persona 2: Tech-Savvy Homeowner

* Age: 25-45
* Income: $30,000 - $70,000
* Education: College-educated
* Occupation: Tech industry professional
* Values: Innovation, convenience, ease of use
* Goals: Automate home energy management, monitor energy usage, optimize energy efficiency
* Pain Points: Limited energy monitoring options, high costs, complex setup

### Persona 3: Budget-Conscious Homeowner

* Age: 25-55
* Income: $20,000 - $50,000
* Education: High school diploma
* Occupation: Service industry worker
* Values: Cost savings, simplicity, ease of use
* Goals: Reduce energy consumption, lower utility bills, increase home value
* Pain Points: High energy bills, lack of visibility into energy usage, complex installation processes
