Residential Solar Platforms Research
==========================

Introduction
------------

The residential solar market is expected to grow significantly in the next few years, with a projected market size of $93.36 billion by 2033 at a CAGR of 14% from 2025 to 2033. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for clean energy sources and the decreasing cost of solar panels.
