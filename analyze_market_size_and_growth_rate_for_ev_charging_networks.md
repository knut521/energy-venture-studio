The market size for EV charging networks is expected to grow from USD 31.69 billion in 2024 to USD 199.77 billion by 2033, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 40.4% from 2026 to 2033. The key players in the market include ChargePoint, EVgo, and Tesla. The customer pain points include the need for more charging stations, faster charging speeds, and more affordable pricing. The gaps in the market include the lack of standardization in charging technology, the need for more investment in charging infrastructure, and the need for more education and awareness about the benefits of electric vehicles.
