Introduction
The Automated Venture Building Studio is a project that aims to run the full venture studio process autonomously with maximum depth for a European energy company, primarily B2C. The studio focuses on various sub-sectors, including home energy apps, residential solar platforms, EV charging networks, community energy trading, smart meter analytics, battery storage services, energy switching tools, virtual power plants, green energy marketplaces, and energy-as-a-service. This gap analysis identifies underserved segments and gaps in the market for each sub-sector and provides recommendations for future ventures.

Home Energy Apps
The home energy app market is a growing segment, with a projected market size of $14.37 billion by 2030. However, there are still gaps in the market, particularly in terms of user experience and functionality. Many home energy apps lack personalized energy usage insights, automated energy-saving recommendations, and seamless integration with smart home devices. To address these gaps, future ventures could focus on developing more user-friendly and feature-rich home energy apps that provide actionable energy-saving recommendations and integrate with popular smart home devices.
