Virtual power plants are a type of distributed energy resource that can provide a range of benefits, including increased efficiency, reduced costs, and improved reliability. They can be used to manage a variety of energy sources, including solar, wind, and energy storage systems. Virtual power plants can also be used to provide demand response services, which can help to balance the grid and reduce the strain on traditional power plants. Overall, virtual power plants have the potential to play a key role in the transition to a more sustainable and resilient energy system.
