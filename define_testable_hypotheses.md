## Venture Hypothesis Definition

The goal of this task is to define testable hypotheses for the top 1 venture concept in the Automated Venture Building Studio for a European energy company (primarily B2C). The hypotheses will be used to validate the venture concept and guide further development.

### Requirements

* Hypotheses are testable and well-written
* All required information is included

### Steps

1. Identify the top 1 venture concept from the previous phase
2. Conduct research on the venture concept using web-search MCP (mcp_call server_name=web-search tool_name=web_search)
3. Define testable hypotheses for the venture concept based on the research findings
4. Write a clear and concise description of each hypothesis, including the expected outcome and any relevant assumptions
5. Validate the hypotheses to ensure they meet the requirements

### Output

A set of testable hypotheses for the top 1 venture concept, including a clear and concise description of each hypothesis and the expected outcome
