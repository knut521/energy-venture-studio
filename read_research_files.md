Based on the research files from Phase 1 of the venture studio process for a European energy company, focusing on B2C, the following are the 5 biggest underserved gaps in the market: 1. Lack of public charging infrastructure for electric vehicles, with many cities and towns lacking sufficient public charging points. 2. Limited availability of fast charging technology, making long-distance travel challenging for electric vehicle owners. 3. Need for better charging network management systems to ensure a seamless charging experience for electric vehicle owners. 4. Requirement for more advanced analytics and machine learning capabilities in smart meter analytics to drive energy savings and efficiency gains. 5. Need for standardized payment protocols and app consolidation platforms in the EV charging network market to simplify the user experience. These gaps present significant opportunities for innovation and growth in the electric vehicle and renewable energy sectors.
