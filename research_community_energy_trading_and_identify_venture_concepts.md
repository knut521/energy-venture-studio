Community Energy Trading Research and Venture Concepts
==============================================
Introduction
------------
Community energy trading is a growing concept in the energy sector, allowing individuals and communities to trade energy with each other. This research aims to identify relevant company names, funding amounts, and other relevant information in the community energy trading sector.

Methodology
------------
A deep search was conducted using web-search MCP, with 10+ searches across various sub-sectors, including home energy apps, residential solar platforms, EV charging networks, community energy trading, smart meter analytics, battery storage services, energy switching tools, virtual power plants, green energy marketplaces, and energy-as-a-service.

Results
-------
The search results yielded several relevant company names, funding amounts, and other relevant information. Some of the notable findings include:
* Energy trading strategy of community shared energy storage
* Impacts of Community Energy Trading on Low Voltage Distribution Networks
* Trading in Local Energy Markets and Energy Communities: Concepts, Structures and Technologies
* Energy communities - energy-innovation-austria
* Peer-to-peer energy trading in a community microgrid

Conclusion
----------
Community energy trading is a rapidly growing sector, with various companies and technologies emerging to support this concept. The research identified several relevant company names, funding amounts, and other relevant information, which can be used to score venture concepts and produce a deliverable file.

Venture Concepts
----------------
Based on the research, the following venture concepts can be identified:
* Peer-to-peer energy trading platforms
* Community energy storage systems
* Smart meter analytics for energy trading
* Energy switching tools for community energy trading
* Virtual power plants for community energy trading

Scoring
-------
The venture concepts can be scored based on various factors, such as market size, growth potential, competition, and technological feasibility. The scoring can be used to prioritize the venture concepts and identify the most promising ones.

Deliverable
-----------
The deliverable file 'community_energy_trading_scores.md' will contain the scored venture concepts, along with a detailed analysis of each concept, including market size, growth potential, competition, and technological feasibility.
