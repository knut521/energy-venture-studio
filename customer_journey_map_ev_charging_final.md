# Comprehensive Customer Journey Map for EV Charging Networks

## Executive Summary
This customer journey map provides a detailed visualization of the European EV owner's experience with charging networks based on comprehensive research from 2024-2025. The analysis reveals critical pain points including reliability issues (30% failure rate), app fragmentation (3-5 apps needed), payment complexity, and user experience barriers that significantly impact EV adoption.

## Market Context
- **Global Market Size**: USD 40.22 billion in 2025, projected to reach USD 238.82 billion by 2033 (CAGR 25.0%)
- **European Infrastructure**: Netherlands (180,000+), Germany (160,000), France (155,000) public charging points
- **Reliability**: Nearly 1 in 3 charging attempts fail despite improved reporting
- **Fragmentation**: Users need 3-5 different apps for various networks
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Public DC fast charging rated 663/1000 points (US Q1 2024)

## Customer Persona: European EV Owner
**Demographics**: Urban/suburban resident, 35-55 years, environmentally conscious, tech-savvy
**Vehicle**: BEV with 250-400 km range
**Primary Goals**: Reliable charging access, cost-effective charging, seamless payment, minimal downtime
**Key Pain Points**: Faulty stations, app fragmentation, payment complexity, range anxiety
**Motivations**: Environmental sustainability, cost savings, convenience, technology adoption

## Customer Journey Stages

### Stage 1: Awareness & Consideration (Pre-Purchase)
**Customer Goal**: Understand charging requirements before EV purchase decision

**Touchpoints**:
1. EV manufacturer websites with charging information
2. Car dealership education sessions
3. Online forums (Reddit r/electricvehicles, Tesla Motors Club)
4. Charging apps (PlugShare, ChargePoint, ABRP)
5. Social media EV communities
6. Friends/family testimonials
7. YouTube review videos
8. Government incentive programs
9. Utility company EV programs
10. Environmental organization resources

**Emotions**:
- **Anxiety**: About charging availability and range limitations
- **Excitement**: About environmental benefits and technology
- **Confusion**: About charging standards (CCS, CHAdeMO, Type 2, Tesla)
- **Concern**: About costs and infrastructure readiness
- **Optimism**: About future transportation solutions

**Behaviors**:
- Researching charging locations along commute routes
- Calculating total ownership costs including charging
- Testing different charging apps and planners
- Visiting public stations to observe usage
- Consulting current EV owners about experiences
- Comparing home charging installation requirements
- Evaluating workplace charging availability
- Researching network coverage in travel areas
- Assessing charging speed requirements
- Investigating payment methods and subscriptions

## Key Pain Points Identified

1. **Reliability Issues**: 30% failure rate for charging attempts
2. **App Fragmentation**: Need for 3-5 different apps
3. **Payment Complexity**: Multiple methods across networks
4. **Pricing Transparency**: Difficult cost comparisons
5. **Availability Information**: Lack of real-time occupancy data
6. **Cross-Network Compatibility**: Limited roaming agreements
7. **Technical Support**: Inconsistent quality and response
8. **Education Gap**: Inadequate onboarding for new owners

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Reliability**: >95% success rate with minimal downtime
2. **Simplicity**: Single app access across networks
3. **Transparency**: Clear pricing and availability information
4. **Convenience**: Seamless payment and authentication
5. **Coverage**: Comprehensive geographic distribution
6. **Speed**: Fast charging matching vehicle technology
7. **Support**: Responsive customer service
8. **Integration**: Navigation and energy management compatibility

## Recommendations for Improvement

1. **Standardization**: Universal payment protocols and cross-network roaming
2. **Reliability Focus**: Predictive maintenance and real-time monitoring
3. **App Consolidation**: Aggregator platforms across multiple networks
4. **Transparency**: Clear pricing, availability, and reliability metrics
5. **Education**: Comprehensive resources for new EV owners
6. **Integration**: Seamless navigation and energy management integration
7. **Community Building**: User communities for experience sharing
8. **Innovation**: V2G and smart charging technologies
