Executive Summary

The Automated Venture Building Studio for a European energy company (primarily B2C) has identified the top 1 venture concept as Community Energy Trading. This concept involves creating a platform that enables communities to trade energy with each other, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources and promoting sustainability.

- Community Energy Trading has the potential to reduce energy costs for consumers and increase revenue for energy producers.
- The platform will utilize blockchain technology to ensure secure and transparent transactions.
- The venture concept aligns with the European energy company's goal of promoting sustainable energy solutions.

Highlights:
- Community Energy Trading has a high potential for growth and adoption in the European market.
- The platform will provide a unique value proposition for consumers and energy producers, setting it apart from existing energy trading platforms.
