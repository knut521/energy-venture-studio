Venture Concepts
1. **Solar Energy Assessment Tool**: Develop a comprehensive solar energy assessment tool that provides accurate and reliable assessments of solar energy potential for residential and commercial properties.
2. **Rooftop Solar Installation**: Offer rooftop solar installation services for residential and commercial properties, providing a turnkey solution for customers to transition to solar energy.
3. **Solar Energy Storage**: Develop and market solar energy storage solutions that enable customers to store excess energy generated by their solar panels, providing a reliable and efficient source of backup power.
4. **Solar Energy Monitoring**: Develop and market solar energy monitoring systems that enable customers to track and optimize their solar energy production, providing real-time data and insights to maximize energy efficiency.
5. **Solar Energy Financing**: Offer solar energy financing solutions that enable customers to purchase and install solar energy systems, providing a range of financing options and incentives to make solar energy more accessible and affordable.
