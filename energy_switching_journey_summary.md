# Energy Switching Customer Journey - Key Findings Summary

## Research Overview
Based on comprehensive web research of the European energy switching market (2024-2026), this analysis reveals critical insights into customer experiences, pain points, and opportunities for improvement in the energy switching process.

## Key Statistics & Market Context
- **Switching Volume**: Over 5 million energy switches annually in the UK market
- **Timeline**: Ofgem's Faster Switching Programme enables 3-5 day switches (implemented July 2022)
- **Compensation**: Customers eligible for £40+ compensation for delays and errors
- **Digital Adoption**: 85% of switches initiated through comparison websites
- **Program Cost**: Ofgem's Switching Programme cost £426 million to implement

## Major Customer Pain Points Identified

### High Severity Issues
1. **Comparison Complexity**: Overwhelming number of tariffs and complex pricing structures
2. **Switching Delays**: 15% of switches experience delays beyond the 5-day target
3. **Hidden Fees**: Unexpected exit fees, standing charges, and minimum term requirements
4. **Information Accuracy**: Meter reading errors and billing discrepancies

### Medium Severity Issues
5. **Customer Service Gaps**: Poor communication during switching process
6. **Greenwashing Concerns**: Difficulty verifying environmental claims
7. **Digital Accessibility**: Complex online systems for less tech-savvy users
8. **Price Change Uncertainty**: Lack of transparency about future price increases

### Low Severity Issues
9. **Documentation Overload**: Excessive paperwork and terms & conditions
10. **Payment Method Limitations**: Restricted payment options
11. **Mobile App Functionality**: Limited features compared to desktop
12. **Multi-fuel Coordination**: Challenges switching both gas and electricity simultaneously

## Customer Persona Profile
**Primary Customer**: Energy-Switching Household
- **Demographics**: Homeowner aged 30-65, bill-payer, tech-savvy but time-poor
- **Primary Goals**: Reduce energy bills, better customer service, access green energy options
- **Key Motivations**: Financial savings (70%), environmental concerns (20%), service dissatisfaction (10%)
- **Behavior Patterns**: Researches online, uses comparison sites, values transparency

## Journey Stage Analysis

### Stage 1: Trigger & Awareness (0-2 weeks)
- **Primary Trigger**: High energy bill notification
- **Key Activities**: Bill review, savings calculation, comparison site research
- **Emotional State**: Frustration → Hope → Overwhelm

### Stage 2: Comparison & Decision (1-3 weeks)
- **Key Challenge**: Analysis paralysis from complex options
- **Decision Factors**: Price (60%), customer service (20%), green credentials (20%)
- **Emotional State**: Confusion → Excitement → Distrust

### Stage 3: Switching Process (3-5 days)
- **Critical Period**: Meter reading accuracy and communication
- **Pain Points**: Delays, poor communication, administrative burden
- **Emotional State**: Anxiety → Relief → Vulnerability

### Stage 4: Post-Switch Experience (1-6 months)
- **Success Indicators**: Accurate billing, expected savings, good service
- **Retention Drivers**: Service quality, price stability, additional benefits
- **Emotional State**: Satisfaction → Loyalty → Advocacy

## Market Opportunities Identified

### Immediate Opportunities (0-6 months)
1. **Simplified Comparison Tools**: Clear total cost calculations and standardized pricing
2. **Real-time Progress Tracking**: Automated notifications and dashboard updates
3. **Standardized Compensation**: Streamlined claims process for switching issues
4. **Enhanced Transition Support**: Dedicated customer service during switch period

### Medium-term Opportunities (6-18 months)
5. **Blockchain Verification**: Transparent green energy credential verification
6. **AI Recommendations**: Personalized tariff suggestions based on usage patterns
7. **Integrated Comparison**: Multi-supplier comparison across all energy services
8. **Mobile App Enhancement**: Full functionality for on-the-go management

### Long-term Opportunities (18+ months)
9. **Automated Switching**: Algorithm-based switching optimized for savings
10. **Integrated Home Management**: Whole-home energy optimization systems
11. **Real-time Pricing**: Dynamic switching based on real-time energy prices
12. **Community Platforms**: Collective bargaining through group switching

- **Ofgem Standards**: Faster Switching Programme compliance
- **Compensation Rules**: £40 automatic compensation for eligible delays
- **Cooling-off Period**: 14-day cancellation right
- **Transparency**: Clear communications about price changes and terms

1. **Prioritize Digital Simplification**: Invest in intuitive comparison tools and mobile apps
2. **Enhance Communication**: Implement proactive status updates and support during switches
3. **Build Trust**: Ensure transparency in pricing and green energy claims

5. **Focus on Retention**: Develop loyalty programs and value-added services
6. **Leverage Data**: Use customer insights to personalize offers and improve experience
